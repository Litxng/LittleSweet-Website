About us

Handcrafted inspiration boba milk tea.

Founded in 2012, Little Sweet is a local American brand. It is Taiwan's original taste of pearl milk tea and is a fashion innovation. It features freshly brewed tea handcrafted make each drink to order. One of the founders is a Taiwanese young man who wants to be authentic. The delicious Taiwanese milk tea is brought to everyone, but you need to wait patiently for every cup of handcrafted tea to order. We hope everyone likes the taste inspiration tea.

DAIRY FREE

We offer dairy-free drinks, enjoy your boba at Little Sweet!
